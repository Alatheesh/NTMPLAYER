DMCA Policy

1. No Hosting Policy

NTM Online respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies fully with the DMCA. We do not host, upload, or store any media files on our own servers.

2. Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright owner and believe that a link on our indexing site directs to your copyrighted work, we are committed to resolving the issue. Since we do not host the files, we cannot remove them from the internet directly, but we will promptly remove the indexing link from our database.

3. How to Contact Us

Please provide the exact URL(s) on NTM Online that link to the infringing material along with proof of copyright ownership.
